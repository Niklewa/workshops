My Mission

To breach the gap between theory and practice. Between the hermetic world of academia and the real-world challenges of thinking and decision-making. Between fast, intuitive judgments and structured, rational reflection.

Hi, I'm Nikodem

I always find it cringy to name myself a philosopher, but that's actually true. (it's not something to eb ashamed of.... as doing that and that... and that what I offer and what interests me the most) As I am an educator, academic and PhD candidate working at the intersection of critical thinking, formal epistemology, and data science. I design and deliver workshops that help individuals and teams think more clearly, reason more rigorously, and communicate more effectively.

My academic background helps me see reasoning as a system — one that can be understood, optimized, and debugged. I'm interested in what we know, why we believe, and how to align our intuitions with evidence and structure.
