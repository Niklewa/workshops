Hi, I'm Nikodem

I always find it a bit cringy to call myself a philosopher—but that's true.

I work at the intersection of critical thinking, logic, and data science, currently pursuing a PhD in philosophy.

Tackling abstract ideas is my passion—and I can't help but bring them to people. My mission is to bridge the gap between theory and practice, turning complex concepts into tools that actually work in everyday life.

I design workshops that help individuals and teams think more clearly, reason more rigorously, and communicate ideas that stick. My focus is on rationality, structured thinking, and clear communication—essential skills in an age of noise and speed.
