Critical Thinking Workshop

Sharpen your reasoning, challenge assumptions, and master the tools of clear thought. This workshop is designed for anyone who wants to think—and communicate—more effectively.

  • Techniques for rigorous, structured thinking
  • How to ask better questions and challenge assumptions
  • Practical tools from logic and philosophy

Workshop Format

  • Interactive sessions (in-person or online)
  • Real-world examples and group exercises
  • Customizable for teams or individuals
  • Duration: 2–6 hours (flexible)
