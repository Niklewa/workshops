About Me

Who I Am

I am Nikodem Lewandowski, an educator, academic, and PhD candidate working at the intersection of critical thinking, formal epistemology, and data science. My goal is to breach the gap between theory and practice, intuition and reason, and ideas and action.

My academic background provides a systemic view of reasoning — allowing me to understand, optimize, and debug thought processes. I am deeply interested in the nature of knowledge, the foundations of belief, and how to align our intuitions with evidence and robust logical structures. I design and deliver workshops that empower individuals and teams to think more clearly, reason more rigorously, and communicate more effectively in both academic and practical settings.
