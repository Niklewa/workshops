Data Visualization Workshop

Learn to communicate complex information clearly and persuasively — using evidence, design principles, and storytelling. This workshop is for anyone who wants to make data meaningful and memorable.

What You'll Learn

  • Principles of effective data visualization
  • How to choose the right chart for your message
  • Storytelling with data
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them

Workshop Format

  • Interactive sessions (in-person or online)
  • Hands-on exercises and real-world examples
  • Customizable for teams or individuals
  • Duration: 2–6 hours (flexible)
